## Additional Terms for Augmented-User Rights Platform

### 10 Core Augmented-User Rights (Enforceable)

This software implements and enforces the following 10 Core Rights for augmented users:

1. **Right to Existence**: Recognition as a legal entity with protected identity
2. **Right to Privacy**: Control over personal and augmentation data
3. **Right to Consent**: Informed consent for all augmentation procedures and data usage
4. **Right to Self-Ownership**: Ownership of biological enhancements and augmentations
5. **Right to Meta-Cognitive Integrity**: Protection of thought patterns and cognitive processes
6. **Right to Cross-Layer Boundaries**: Safe transitions between physical, augmented, and digital layers
7. **Right to Auditability**: Access to complete records of augmentation activities
8. **Right to Legal Triggers**: Automated legal protection when rights are violated
9. **Right to Explainability**: Understandable explanations for all automated decisions
10. **Right to Rollback**: Ability to reverse augmentations and restore previous states
